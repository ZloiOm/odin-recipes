Lasagna

Description

A hearty Italian dish, the taste of which has won the hearts of gourmets all over the world. Finely chopped meat filling with béchamel sauce between layers of thin dough, sprinkled with different types of cheese and Italian herbs, baked until golden crispy.

Ingredients

  • Butter - 60 g
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Wheat flour - 3 tablespoons
  • Milk - 750 ml
  • Ground meat - 600 g
  • Tomato sauce - 600 g
  • Salt - to taste
  • Ground black pepper - to taste
  • Dry lasagne sheets from ready-made dough - 10 pieces
  • Hard cheese (parmesan, cheddar) - 500 g

Cooking steps

  1. Place butter in a saucepan, pour in vegetable oil and melt the mixture over medium heat. Add flour one spoon at a time and stir with a whisk to avoid lumps.
  2. Reduce heat, pour in milk and simmer until the mixture reaches the consistency of low-fat sour cream. You will get a béchamel sauce.
  3. Heat a frying pan, pour olive oil into it, put the mince and fry it until half-ready. Add tomato sauce, sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Grease the pan with butter and pour some of the sauce you made in the saucepan onto the bottom. Spread the sauce over the bottom.
  5. Place the lasagne sheets in one layer, then the minced meat on top, and grated cheese on top. Pour the bechamel sauce over the cheese. Place the sheets, minced meat, cheese and sauce on top again.
  6. Spread the last layer of sheets with sauce and sprinkle with cheese. Let stand for 7-10 minutes, then bake the lasagne for 30 minutes at 180 degrees.

The dish is ready! Enjoy your meal!
