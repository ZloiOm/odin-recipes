Children`s-salad

Description

Healthy and very tasty salad with chicken meat. This salad is perfect for a children's party, as it has a delicate and sweet taste.

Ingredients

  • Iceberg lettuce leaves - 1 bunch
  • Sweet pickled corn - 300-400 gr
  • Smoked chicken meat - 300-400 gr
  • Chicken eggs - 4-5 pieces
  • Rusks from sweet white bread or bun - 100 g
  • Mayonnaise sauce - to taste

Cooking steps

  1. Prepare a large bowl for mixing the ingredients.
  2. Let's boil the eggs hard. Wash the eggs, put them in a saucepan, pour in cold water and put them on the stove to boil. After the water boils, boil the eggs for 10-12 minutes. Then drain the hot water and pour cold water over the eggs until they cool completely.
  3. While the eggs are boiling, cut the iceberg lettuce leaves into strips into a bowl. Pour the corn and croutons into the bowl. Cut the chicken into cubes and add to the bowl.
  4. Peel the cooled boiled eggs. Cut the eggs into cubes and add them to the bowl with the other ingredients.
  5. Add sauce to taste, stir.

The dish is ready! Enjoy your meal!
